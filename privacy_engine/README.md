\# Privacy Engine



A reusable privacy filtering and PII anonymization module for AI systems.



\## Features



\- Email masking

\- Phone number masking

\- Card number masking

\- PII detection

\- Privacy-first preprocessing



\## Example



Input:



My email is test@gmail.com and my phone number is 9876543210



Output:



My email is \[EMAIL\_HIDDEN] and my phone number is \[PHONE\_HIDDEN]
